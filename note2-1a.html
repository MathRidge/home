Shelf Scale Manual

Large shelf expressions are easier when the top shelf splits into friendly chunks. Reduce each chunk, then add the counts to get a cleaner answer.

1. Split top shelfChoose a big friendly chunk.
2. Reduce chunksEach piece works with the same bottom shelf.
3. Add countsJoin the chunk counts into one clean answer.

03 Pick a Friendly Chunk

A friendly chunk is chosen, not guessed. It should be big, below the top shelf, and easy for the bottom shelf.

1. Biguse a chunk that clears a lot of shelf
2. Belowdo not take more shelf than you have
3. Easythe bottom shelf should work cleanly

06 Common Mistakes

These mistakes are normal. Catching them is part of learning the route.

Too high150 is friendly, but it is above 141. Too much shelf.
Not friendly140 is below 141, but 140 does not clean nicely with 3.
Forgot leftover141 → 120 still leaves 21 waiting.
Forgot to add120 gives 40. 21 gives 7. Do not stop at 40: 40 + 7 → 47.

Split Shelf Division

Large shelf numbers are easier when you split them into friendly chunks. This is division by distribution: divide each friendly piece, then add the group counts.

1. Friendly chunkChoose a close value the group can divide.
2. Leftover shelfSubtract to see what remains.
3. Add group countsEach chunk gives part of the answer.

01 Why Split the Shelf?

Some students know small division facts but freeze when the shelf number is large. Split Shelf turns a big value into friendlier pieces.

A friendly chunk is not random. It is close, below the shelf number, and easy for the group to divide.

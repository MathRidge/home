The Sign Outside the Box

One focus: every answer has an outside sign and an inside operation. Find the bigger size, keep its sign, then use the other term to choose add or subtract.

1. Find bigThe bigger size carries the outside sign.
2. Check otherSame sign adds. Different sign subtracts.
3. Say the answerOutside sign + inside size.

01 Start with One Term

A term has two parts. The sign tells the direction. The number tells the size.

sign+positive direction
size4how far

Focus cue: do not separate the sign from the number. Read −3 as “negative three,” not just “three.”

02 The First Plus Can Hide

If the first number has no sign, it is still positive. The plus sign is quiet, not missing.

4→+4
7→+7
Before solving, give every term a sign in your head. This keeps the next step calmer.

03 Same Signs Stay on One Team

When both signs match, the terms move the same direction. Add the sizes. Keep the shared sign outside the box.

Positive team

Negative team

04 Different Signs: Bigger Size Leads

When signs are different, compare the sizes first. The bigger size gives the final outside sign.

Example A

+3 −8

8 is larger than 3. The 8 is negative, so the outside sign is negative.

−8 − 3→−5

Example B

−4 +6

6 is larger than 4. The 6 is positive, so the outside sign is positive.

+6 − 4→+2
Different signssubtract sizesbigger sign goes outside

05 The Bigger Sign and Operation Rule

Use this rhythm for any two signed terms. The bigger size gives the sign; the other term decides the operation.

Find BigCompare the two sizes. The larger size leads.
Keep SignWrite the sign carried by the larger size outside.
Check OtherIf the other sign matches, add the sizes.
If ChangedIf the other sign changes, subtract the sizes.
Two steps: bigger size gives the outside sign, then the other term chooses add or subtract.

06 Lesson 1-1 Summary

Every signed-term problem has two jobs: choose the answer direction, then choose the answer size.

TermA term is sign plus size. Read them together.
Outside SignThe bigger size carries the final sign.
OperationThe other term tells you add or subtract.
Final AnswerSay the sign first, then the size.
Short version: Big size sign outside. Other same: add. Other different: subtract. Answer sign first.

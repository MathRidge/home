Chapter 1

Numbers Come with Signs
Read Them Together

This is Number Town. Every number lives here.

Every number wears a badge.

Plus means: “I walk forward.”
Minus means: “I walk backward.”

Numbers also have size.

A number is a sign and a size…
together.

One day, +4 made a mistake.

“I see a 3!” said +4.
“I will walk forward to say Hi!”

But -3 was walking backward.
BUMP!

“You forgot my sign,” said -3.

+10 came to help.

“Always read the sign
and the number together.”

Same sign… walk together.

They become bigger.
+4 + +2 = +6

Different signs… stop and look.

Who is bigger?

The bigger one leads.
+6 + (−3) = +3

You are not wrong for who you are.

Some numbers walk with you.
Some do not.

If you stay with the wrong group too long…

They can slow you down…
or pull you back.

You can choose who you walk with.

“I understand now,” said +5.
“I read the whole number.”

Sign + Number
Read them together.

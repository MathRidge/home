About Math Ridge

I, Kuan-Yuan (a.k.a. Kevin) Huang, with about 10 years of experience specialize in helping students truly understand math; not just memorize it. My approach focuses on breaking down concepts so students gain confidence and independence in problem-solving.

With experience guiding students through Algebra, Geometry, Algebra II, Pre-Calculus, and AP Calculus, I help turn frustration into clarity and measurable progress.

My goal is simple: build strong foundations so students succeed long-term, not just on the next test.

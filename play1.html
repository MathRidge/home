The Term Stone

The lower trail is testing the rule from your manual. Find the bigger size first. Its sign goes outside. Then look at the other term: same signs add, different signs subtract. Start when you are ready — the climb timer begins after you press the button.

What makes 1-1 different: you build the whole signed-size box yourself: bigger size, bigger sign, operation, box placement, and final answer.

Term Stone challenge:

Follow the steps: bigger size, bigger sign, operation, box setup, final answer.

Step 1: Which size is bigger?

Compare size only right now. For example, -8 carries size 8, and +3 carries size 3.

Step 2: What sign does the bigger size carry?

Tap the sign directly in front of the bigger size.

Step 3: What operation should happen inside?

Look at the other term. Same sign uses addition. Different sign uses subtraction.

Choose the sign relationship.

Step 4: Fill in the box

The sign and operation are locked in. Type the bigger size first, then the smaller size.

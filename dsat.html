DSAT Prep

The Digital SAT (DSAT) is a pivotal step in your academic journey, influencing college admissions and scholarship opportunities.

While many students may be familiar with the material, the unique format and question styles of the DSAT often catch even the best-prepared off guard.

My course is designed to bridge that gap, giving you not just the knowledge but also the test-taking experience to excel.

Together, we'll address common pitfalls like careless mistakes, unexpected question phrasing, and the pressure of a timed environment.


A Key Tip for Success:

One of the most effective tools for improving your performance is a pocket-sized notepad.

During the course, I encourage students to jot down every mistakes they make; whether it's a miscalculation, a misinterpretation of the question, or simply forgetting a key concept.

This notepad serves as a quick reference guide to track what you've learned, highlight areas of improvement, and remind you to avoid repeating the same errors.

Reviewing this notepad regularly helps solidify your progress and ensures you're continually refining your approach.

What We'll Cover:

My comprehensive DSAT preparation course focuses on the core areas assessed in the exam, ensuring you are fully prepared for every challenge:

  1. Pre-Algebra: Strengthen your foundation with number properties, basic operation, and working with exponential equations.

  2. Problem Solving and Data Analysis: Master interpreting graphs, analyzing trends, and solving real-world math problems.

  3. Algebra: Build confidence in solving linear equations, inequalities, and system of equations.

  4. Advance Math: Tackle quadratic equation, composite functions, transformation, and higher-level algebraic concept.

  5. Geometry and Trigonometry: Focus on essential concepts like angles and triangle, volume and density, circles, and trigonometry ratios crucial for DSAT success.

SAT Prep

The Digital SAT (DSAT) is a pivotal step in your academic journey, influencing college admissions and scholarship opportunities.

While many students may be familiar with the material, the unique format and question styles of the DSAT often catch even the best-prepared off guard.

My course is designed to bridge that gap, giving you not just the knowledge but also the test-taking experience to excel.

Together, we'll address common pitfalls like careless mistakes, unexpected question phrasing, and the pressure of a timed environment.


A Key Tip for Success:

One of the most effective tools for improving your performance is a pocket-sized notepad.

During the course, I encourage students to jot down every mistakes they make; whether it's a miscalculation, a misinterpretation of the question, or simply forgetting a key concept.

This notepad serves as a quick reference guide to track what you've learned, highlight areas of improvement, and remind you to avoid repeating the same errors.

Reviewing this notepad regularly helps solidify your progress and ensures you're continually refining your approach.

What We'll Cover:

My comprehensive DSAT preparation course focuses on the core areas assessed in the exam, ensuring you are fully prepared for every challenge:

  1. Pre-Algebra: Strengthen your foundation with number properties, basic operation, and working with exponential equations.

  2. Problem Solving and Data Analysis: Master interpreting graphs, analyzing trends, and solving real-world math problems.

  3. Algebra: Build confidence in solving linear equations, inequalities, and system of equations.

  4. Advance Math: Tackle quadratic equation, composite functions, transformation, and higher-level algebraic concept.

  5. Geometry and Trigonometry: Focus on essential concepts like angles and triangle, volume and density, circles, and trigonometry ratios crucial for DSAT success.

Subject I Cover

I offer tutoring in a wide range of math topics, starting from fundamental concepts and gradually building toward advanced applications:

  • Course Math (Grade 3-6):
    Foundational arithmetic, fractions, decimals, percentages, basic geometry, and word problems. This stage prepares student for higher level math by strengthening their core skills.
  • Pre-Algebra:
    Number properties, ratio, proportions, exponents, and introductory equations.
  • Algebra I:
    Linear equations, inequalities, quadratic functions, polynomials, and graphing.
  • Geometry:
    Lines, angels, triangles, circles, coordinate geometry, and basic proofs.
  • Algebra II:
    Complex numbers, logarithms, system of equations, and advanced polynomial functions.
  • Pre-Calculus
    Trigonometric functions, sequences and series, vectors, and introductory calculus concepts.
  • Trigonometry
    Advance trigonometric identities, graphing trig functions, and solving real-world applications.
  • AP Calculus
    Limits, derivatives, integrals, and applications of calculus concepts (AB and BC).

Each course is designed to align with school curriculums while offering deeper insights to ensure mastery of the material.

About Me

Born in Taichung City, Taiwan, I, Kuan-Yuan also known as Kevin Huang, grew up in Guam, where my family operated a long-liner fishing and international exporting business.

This unique upbringing instilled in me a strong work ethic and a curiosity for understanding the world through numbers.

My journey eventually brought me to Washington, where I pursued a Physics degree at the University of Washington Seattle campus while enlisted in the United States Army Reserve.

Serving eight fulfilling years in the military not only shape my discipline and leadership skills but also deepened my appreciation for education as a tool for growth and opportunity.

After my military service, I took on the challenge of managing my family's business in Palau and Yap, balancing leadership responsibilities with my growing passion for teaching math.

It was during this time I discovered how much joy I found in helping others understand complex concepts and overcome learning obstacles.

Whether solving a challenging problem or guiding someone to a "lightbulb moment," I realized that teaching math was not just a profession for me, it was a calling.

Now, back in the mainland US, I am thrilled to embark on this new chapter of teaching while raising my family with my loving wife and two wonderful children.

My goal is to give students the tools and mindset they need to succeed in math and beyond--skills I wish I had been taught at an earlier age.

Teaching is more than sharing knowledge for me; it's about empowering the next generation to embrace challenges and achieve their full potential.

I look forward to meeting you and being part of your journey to math mastery!
